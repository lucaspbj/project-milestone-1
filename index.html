Welcome to Waterford, the oldest city in Ireland

Waterford is a city in County Waterford, located in the south-east of Ireland. It is the oldest city in the Republic of Ireland and the fifth most populous. Situated at the head of Waterford Harbour, it is part of the province of Munster.

Picture yourself standing on the quays in Waterford city, breathing in the salty air. It's easy to imagine the busy ships loading and unloading cargo in the 1700s. Trade with Newfoundland attracted wealthy merchants who settled here in the late 18th century, transforming the city into a major Irish port and sparking a building boom.
